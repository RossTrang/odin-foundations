Massaman Curry (Rich and Flavorful Curry)

Description

Massaman Curry is a rich and flavorful Thai curry that is believed to have originated from Persian and Indian influences. It is a complex dish with a blend of sweet, savory, and spicy flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of massaman curry paste
  • 400 milliliters of coconut milk
  • 400 grams of potatoes, cubed
  • 400 grams of beef, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cardamom
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cloves

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, stir-fry until softened.
  2. Add massaman curry paste and stir-fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add coconut milk, potatoes, beef, fish sauce, sugar, cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender and beef is cooked through.
  4. Serve with rice or roti.
