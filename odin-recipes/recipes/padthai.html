Pad Thai (Stir-Fried Rice Noodles)

Description

Pad Thai is a beloved Thai dish known for its sweet, sour, and savory flavors. It is a popular street food and a national dish of Thailand.

Ingredients:

  • 200 grams of rice noodles, soaked in warm water
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 100 grams of bean sprouts
  • 100 grams of chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons of tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili pepper, sliced

Steps:

  1. Drain the rice noodles and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok over medium heat. Add garlic and chili pepper, stir-fry until fragrant.
  3. Add beaten eggs and scramble until cooked.
  4. Add rice noodles, bean sprouts, and green onions. Stir-fry until combined.
  5. Add tamarind paste, fish sauce, and sugar. Stir-fry until well combined.
  6. Garnish with chopped peanuts and serve immediately.
