Tom Yum Goong (Spicy and Sour Shrimp Soup)

Description

Tom Yum Goong is a beloved Thai dish known for its vibrant flavors and aromatic broth. It is a perfect balance of sweet, sour, spicy, and salty, making it a truly unforgettable experience.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 liters of water
  • 1 lemongrass stalk, bruised
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • 4 galangal slices
  • 4 Thai chilies, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • tablespoon of lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 200 grams of shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 100 grams of mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped cilantro

Steps:

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add lemongrass, kaffir lime leaves, galangal, and chilies. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add fish sauce, lime juice, and sugar. Taste and adjust seasonings.
  4. Add shrimp and mushrooms. Cook until shrimp are pink and cooked through.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve immediately.
